Sprinklify App for truck drivers

This app was created with a specific group for users to satistfy their daily needs.

Services Provided
  • Discovery & Strategy

Overview

To design a app for a truck driver whose daily job is to deliver jobs is to deliver heavy duty machines from one place to another for different needs of the user or on the specific need of the employee.

My role: I did the Analysis wherein I worked on the Case Study, User study and Market Research and created Personas, Empathy Maps and Customer Journeys. I also did the branding and visual design.

“Be sure to check out the live preview and let us know your thoughts in the comments section below.”

My Process.

User Experience.
  • Wireframes
  • Prototyping
  • User Testing
User Interface.
  • Digitalising Wireframes
  • Adding Functionalities in the UI
  • Constructing the main UI
Approach

Due to the time constraints, I quickly rushed to few truck drivers and had small talk regarding as to what do they do all day and how do they manage it all. This way I came with valuable insights and came up with a few personas.

They are those who are really hard workers, who work beyond their limits to fulfil the needs of their family. These are the drivers who love interacting with new technology but cannot due to illiteracy.
